CIS community about Natus Vincere G2A future

 

While  Natus Vincere G2A management is searching for the new players players for our Dota 2 roster, we decided to ask several questions on the future of  Na`Vi to a prominent figures of CIS community and to consider possible candidates.

 

We asked the following questions:

 

Now Natus Vincere Dota 2 line-up comprises of 2 players Dendi and GeneRaL. The team needs 3 more players. Which players do you see in the line-up and whom would you advise to take? What would be the optimal Na`Vi roster in the current situation?

 

  • The first to answer was popular CIS caster  Vladimir "maelstorm" Kuzminov:

 

"I don't know how real it is to accomplish, but it seems to me that the roster should comprise of CIS players exclusively. Everyone [in CIS - translator's note] is waiting for the CIS roster, no matter how hard it would be to create and promote one.

 

There are tons of options, but acquiring good players doesn't imply creating a good team. There's no use in assembling a clock of high quality gears, if they just don't fit each other. The clock simply won't work. In the same time even cast iron gears would do good, if they fit.

 

First of all a leader should be determined, which has always been a huge problem both in the CIS and Na`Vi. Danil, it's your time".

 

 

  • The former streamer of  Natus Vincere  Aren "VeRsuta" Zurabian was laconic:

 

"I think that the ideal candidates as per moment are Ilya "Illidan" Pivtsaev and the teammate of Dendi and GeneRaL in Team Ukraine  Andrew "ALWAYSWANNAFLY" Bondarenko. Getting SoNNeikO back would be good too. It's a pity to lose the player of such level".

 

  • The former player of the Born to Win, the current caster and analyst  Sergey "ARS-ART" Revin didn't name any candidates, but suggested a different approach:

 

"Before completing the line-up, I would mover Dendi on 1st position. I won't name those players that should be taken, since those players I wish to see in the line-up are the members of the other teams now and there's no point in disturbing them.

 

I'd prefer the young players from pubs. There are many of them in pubs, for every position. Why so? Because there are many players now. This is not how it was back in 2011-2012, when you could count top players on one hand and had no choice. So... take youngster, take a coach... and learn how to walk again.

 

You can't stay in top all the time. Sometimes it happens so that the players get older. You need to mow down everything".

 

 

  • The creator of Dota 2 videos  Igor "Bad_Playa" Gorevoy was rather unequivocal about the mixed line-up:

 

"I can hardly suggest anyone, but intuitively, it seems to me that the roster comprising of GeneRaL, Dendi, ALWAYAWANNAFLY, fng (artstyle) and Ramzes666 could become the team. I am pretty sure, they're unlikely to succeed with the foreigners. Any CIS team is".

 

  • An eSports enthusiast  Alexander "derrick_deny" Hitrov decided to offer a somewhat fantastic option for a new roster. Anyway, why not?

 

"Let's try to fantasize a bit. Let's base our suggestion on the assumption that the roster will be formed after The Boston Major. We'll be watching Digital Chaos during the tournament. Let's assume (by no means wish) DC fails the fall major. In this case, there's a chance the roster would undergo the reshuffle. In this case, I'd be pleased to see the following roster of Na`Vi: Dendi, GeneRaL, Resolut1on, MiSeRy + 1. Rasmus often visits Kyiv. He's perceived as "native" by the local Dota 2 community.

 

Who would be the fifth player? Roman Resolut1on highly estimated Saksa as a support. So one option is to take the three of them from DC. However, I don't know what Martin thinks about it. Taking a CIS player for the second support role seems more preferable. Whom to take? That is an open question. Maybe there's a chance to bring SoNNeikO back?".

 

 

  • The caster of StarLadder studio  Alexey "BafiK" Bafadarov suggested a rather powerful line-up of CIS players for  Na`Vi:

 

"Similar to many other casters, I expected the complete rebuild of the team. That didn't happen with Dendi and GeneRal remaining in the team, which will again base on Danil. I hope he still has motivation for new achievements, which is in my opinion a key moment. There's no doubt GeneRaL craves to win. As concerns the remaining three positions, there aren't many powerful free agents on the market. For support-position we can take a look at ALWAYSWANNAFLY and search a good teammate for him. However, things are much more complicated with carry-positions. The most powerful players are engaged in serious and ambitious rosters, so luring any of them would be hard. I had assumptions that after The Boston Major the transfer of Resolut1on to Na`Vi is possible, but much depends on the major's results and the wish of Roman.

 

DkPhobos who hasn't played in pro teams for a while. We might see him in Na`Vi, if he's capable of playing on the fourth position. There are different options, but if the team aims the top results, it needs the most powerful players, perhaps even it should by them out.

 

My option for Na`Vi roster: Resolution (fn), Dendi, GeneRal, DkPhobos (Miposhka), ALWAYSWANNAFLY (KingR, CemaTheSlayer). I mentioned the good players in the brackets, but it will be really hard to get them for Na`Vi. Apart from the wish of these players (if they have any), the team will have to pay a pretty big sum. I think everything will be sold after The Boston Major, before that the organization shouldn't rush and make decisions.

 

Good luck, Natus Vincere!»

 

  • The caster  Dmitry "Droog" Chumachenko suggested to take the foreigners:

 

"I think we need an "exterior" help. Make a big effort but take foreign players. Try reboot. KuroKy, Puppey and MATUMBAMAN as carries. I am almost sure that none of the CIS players will fit and stay for a long time. Otherwise he will fit, but have no effect in terms of results. To support my idea: KuroKy, Puppey and MATUMBAMAN didn't get to the major".

 

 

  • RuHub caster  Victor "GodHunt" Volkov wants to see CIS players in our line-up:

 

"I think that Resolut1on would be the most interesting option for Natus Vincere.He's capable of easily becoming an IGL the team needs so much. Furthermore, you should try fng again. He's a free agent now and he can become useful for the Born to Win. The last candidate is yoky-, who should develop his talent. Certainly, such line-up would have some problems with the positioning on the battlefield, but the experienced pros could come to the agreement".

 

  • The last, but not the least is analyst  Yaroslv "NS" Kuznetsov, who suggested creating a junior team rather than stick to the established representatives of pro scene:

 

"It is hard to suggest the players for the first roster. You can take any of the prominent player, but frankly speaking it's like fortune telling with coffee grounds. It might or might not succeed.

 

I'd suggest Na`Vi to create junior teams. You can work with the players in these teams and take the best of them to the main roster. Chinese practice proves this method to be the most adequate one: juniors successfully take the free positions in the main line-ups. Sometimes a junior team becomes an autonomous battle unity and succeeds at many tournaments. 

 

It seems to me that in the situation of Na`Vi, where management have tried everything they could, the development of junior team is at least not mistaken. What hype could be created in community. There are many gamers who crave to play for Na`Vi, even in junior team.

 

There's a big chance to never collect the team of the established players even if you continue doing it very eagerly".

 

Dear fans, we'll be glad to hear your opinion. Share your suggestions and leave the names of the candidates you deem best for  Natus Vincere in the comments below. It might happen so that your option will hit!
